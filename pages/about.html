My Story
Hi, My name is Akeeb Ayomami Wasiu, a native of Osun State, Nigeria and i am the CEO of Ayrem services.

I attended Ladoke Akintola university of technology Ogbomoso and graduated with second class upper credit honours in urban and regional planning. In addition to this, I was trained as a c raftsman from the early stage of my life till I completed my university education.
It is clear that no Government can employ all the residents of her country, therefore, citizens have to be self-employed in other to cater for their daily demands in a socially acceptable way and manner. having this at heart, I was determined not to work under anybody except for consultation cases. Although in course of hustle tatics, i have worked as an attaché for several private firms and government agencies but I have never lost my dream of becoming my own boss someday, This dream, i have lightened up as a ray of hope in the heart of many citizens, that we still have lots of good people with several potentials in Nigeria.
In addition to my selfless activities, , I assisted a couple of persons, both those in Nigeria and in diaspora, supervised their project efficiently and got their planning and architectural approval in most of the states in Nigeria, all these i did in line with many other professions in the building industry.
